Credit Risk and Loan Default Prediction

Business Objective:

Lending Club is a company that pairs investors with those in need of a loan. Since investors are concerned with whether the borrower will be able to pay off their loan, it is important to predict the likelihood of defaulting based on characteristics of the borrower. The goal of this analysis is to use deep learning to predict whether a borrower defaults or pays their loan.

Dataset Information:

The dataset can be obtained from Kaggle - Lending Club Loan Data: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/adarshsng/lending-club-loan-data-csv

It contains information about over 2.2 million loans with 145 features.
